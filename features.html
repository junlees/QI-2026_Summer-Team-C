● A closer look

Every feature, explained in depth.

From on-device pose estimation to crew-wide pace sync — here's exactly how Pace Runner turns raw motion into coaching you can act on mid-run.

🏃
🏃

Real-Time Pose Detection

Pace Runner runs on-device pose estimation directly in your browser, tracking 21 key body points at up to 30fps — no wearables, no extra hardware, no round-trip to a server.

  • Tracks posture, arm swing, stride length, and foot strike
  • Works from any camera angle — side, front, or trailing
  • Runs fully on-device, so your video never leaves your phone
📊
📊

Form Analysis

Raw pose data is instantly translated into the metrics that actually predict injury and slowdown — overstriding, cadence drift, and left/right asymmetry.

  • Session-by-session form score out of 100
  • Trend lines across weeks, not just single runs
  • Flags the one thing most worth fixing next
🎧
🎧

Live Coaching Cues

Audio and haptic nudges arrive the moment your form drifts, so you can correct it without breaking stride to check a screen.

  • Configurable cue sensitivity and frequency
  • Haptic-only mode for quiet group runs
  • Cues tuned to your current effort level, not a fixed script
⏱️
⏱️

Smart Pacemaking

Set a goal pace or a race split, and Pace Runner recalculates your target in real time based on remaining distance and current effort.

  • Negative-split and even-split pacing modes
  • Auto-adjusts for elevation change
  • Live delta to goal, updated every stride
👥
👥

Crew Mode

Sync pace and form data across your whole running crew, so pacers, coaches, and teammates all see the same live picture of the group.

  • Shared live view of everyone's pace and position
  • Pacer tools for keeping the group together
  • Coach dashboard to flag runners who need attention
📈
📈

Progress Over Time

Every run adds to a running history of your form and pace, with coaching recommendations that adapt as you improve.

  • Weekly and monthly progress views
  • Personalized drills based on your weakest metric
  • Exportable data for coaches and training logs
